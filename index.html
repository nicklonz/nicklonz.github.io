üìê What Are Fibonacci Numbers?

The Fibonacci sequence starts with 0 and 1. Each number after is the sum of the two before it. So it goes: 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21...

üî¨ Real-World Applications

  • Engineering: The spiral pattern occurs in turbine design and signal processing.
  • Finance: Fibonacci retracement is used in technical analysis of stock trends.
  • Nature: Found in pinecones, sunflower seeds, and the branching of trees.
